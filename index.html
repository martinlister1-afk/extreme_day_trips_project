Extreme Day Trips

We believe adventure shouldn’t wait for annual leave or a week-long holiday. Extreme Day Trips is all about turning one ordinary day into something unforgettable.

Our mission is simple: make extreme travel accessible, achievable, and ridiculously fun for people with busy lives.

Sample Extreme Day Trip Ideas

Here are a few example concepts to show what’s possible in a single day:

  • City-Hopping Challenge: Start with breakfast in Paris, take a mid-morning train to Brussels for waffles, then hop to Amsterdam for a canal tour and dinner—all in 24 hours.
  • Adrenaline Rush: Begin with sunrise paragliding over the Alps, followed by a midday canyoning adventure, and finish with a night-time zipline under the stars.
  • Cultural Sprint: Explore three different countries’ capitals in one day—visit Rome’s ancient sites at dawn, have lunch in Vatican City, then catch an evening flight to Athens for a sunset view of the Acropolis.
